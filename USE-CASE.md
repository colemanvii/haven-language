## Use Case: Slowing a Decision

Situation:
A decision is being pushed with urgency.
Multiple opinions are present.
Tension is rising.

Typical response:
- Debate
- Justification
- Moral framing
- Pressure to decide now

Haven Language response:
“I don’t have enough signal yet.”
“There’s pressure, but no immediate danger.”
“Let’s slow this until it makes sense.”

What changes:
- Urgency drops
- Nervous systems settle
- Clarity improves
- Better decisions emerge

Nothing was argued.
Nothing was forced.

Orientation did the work.
